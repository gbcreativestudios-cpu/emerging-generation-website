Stella Orji-Dada is a visionary leader and advocate. Recognizing the unique systemic challenges faced by newcomer youth and immigrants, she founded The Emerging Generation to bridge the gap between arrival and true belonging. Her work focuses on structural inclusion, sustainable mentorship networks, physical well-being, and community empowerment.
